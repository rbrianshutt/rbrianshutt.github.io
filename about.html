Hello

A little about me...


I’m a self taught developer/programmer that is passionate about technology and learning. My emphasis has been front-end, but I am learning the back-end as well. I come from a strong background in management and working with teams to achieve results.


My coding journey began in 2020 when I was furloughed from my job due to Covid-19. I committed to learning new skills. The more I learned the more passionate I became about learning tech. I started with the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript using Visual Studio Code.


I had the opportunity to get back to work in June of 2020 , but it was in another state and I would be living on site. Everything was locked down, and I was away from my family which gave me the opportunity to further my studies in development and programming. Over time, I learned React, some Redux, and Git.


After my 6 month contract was up in December 2020, I was able to come back home to work in Missouri. Throughout 2021 I gained exposure to SQL, .Net Core, #C, Azure, Visual Studio, and various styling languages and frameworks including SASS, Bootstrap, Material UI, and Tailwind CSS. I also spent time learning data structures and algorithms


A little about me personally: I enjoy music, anything to do with hiking and mountains, and spending time with my wife and kids. I love a good cup of coffee or tea and reading, but lately I’m more focused on reading and learning programming.


While I have been a hobbyist in tech over the past couple of years, I am looking to transition into tech professionally. My goal is to become a full stack developer/programmer.
